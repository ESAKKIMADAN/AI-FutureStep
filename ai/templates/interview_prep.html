Common Interview Questions

1. Tell me about yourself.

Answer: Provide a brief overview of your education, experience, skills, and career goals.

2. What are your strengths?

Answer: Highlight key skills that match the job role, with examples.

3. What are your weaknesses?

Answer: Share a minor weakness and how you are improving it.

4. Why do you want to work here?

Answer: Align your goals with the company's mission and vision.

5. Where do you see yourself in 5 years?

Answer: Share your future plans that show growth and commitment.

Hard / Advanced Interview Questions

1. Explain a challenging project you worked on and how you handled obstacles.

Answer: Describe the complexity, challenges faced, your solution approach, and results.

2. How do you stay updated with the latest trends in technology?

Answer: Mention online courses, certifications, GitHub projects, blogs, etc.

3. How would you optimize a slow-performing application?

Answer: Identify bottlenecks (database, algorithms), optimize queries/code, and use caching.

4. Describe SOLID principles and why they are important.

Answer: Explain Single Responsibility, Open-Closed, Liskov Substitution, Interface Segregation, Dependency Inversion.

5. How would you design a scalable URL shortening service (like Bit.ly)?

Answer: Discuss API design, hashing, database scaling, caching, load balancing.

Coding Challenges

1. Two Sum

Given an array of integers nums and an integer target, return indices of the two numbers such that they add up to target.

Solve on LeetCode
2. Longest Substring Without Repeating Characters

Given a string s, find the length of the longest substring without repeating characters.

Solve on LeetCode
3. Merge Two Sorted Lists

Merge two sorted linked lists and return it as a new sorted list.

Solve on LeetCode
4. Maximum Subarray

Find the contiguous subarray which has the largest sum and return its sum.

Solve on LeetCode
5. Word Ladder

Given two words (beginWord and endWord), and a dictionary's word list, find the shortest transformation sequence from beginWord to endWord.

Solve on LeetCode
